I am here<br>
Waiting for you<br>
You are there<br>
Waiting for him<br>
It's all about choices
<br><br>
My pen bleeds<br>
Inner voice seeps<br>
Mist on lenses<br>
Say it all<br>
It's all about choices
<br><br>
More like a dream<br>
Sweetest os the sweet<br>
Freezing speed of life<br>
You are there<br>
Not with me<br>
But happier with him<br>
'Cause it's all about choices
