<center>
When I was young<br>
I used to believe<br>
People hide feelings<br>
It’s so strange
<br><br>
I met you<br>
Eye met you<br>
The world whispered<br>
And I fumbled<br>
Grasping for words<br>
Easing me there<br>
Was that smile<br>
Frozen moments<br>
Void speed of life<br>
Tearing me apart<br>
Killing from within<br>
There was that feeling<br>
The feeling that I hide
<br><br>
Now I understand<br>
Now I believe<br>
People hide feelings<br>
It’s not so strange.
